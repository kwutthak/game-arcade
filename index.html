Whack-A-Mole

Join Kuromi in a fun-filled game of Whack-A-Mole! Keep your eyes peeled as Kuromi pops up from her cozy burrow. Use your quick reflexes to whack her with a smile and earn points. But be careful, Kuromi is playful and might surprise you with her mischievous antics! Let's have a blast with Kuromi in this delightful game adventure!

Memory Cards

Test your memory with our delightful Memory Cards game! Join your favorite Sanrio characters as they peek out from behind their cards. Immerse yourself in the joy of matching pairs as you flip and match cards to reveal the lovable characters hidden within. But beware, as you venture deeper into the game, the challenge increases, testing your memory skills to the fullest!

Dino Runner

Take control of Keroppi, the lovable green frog, as he embarks on an exhilarating adventure through vibrant landscapes filled with challenges. With a simple tap of the spacebar, guide Keroppi as he leaps gracefully over obstacles. Jump higher and higher to reach new heights, but watch out for tricky traps that stand in your way!
